About Us

Fossil Contracting is a leading Zimbabwean civil engineering and infrastructure development company specializing in road construction, earthworks, contract mining, asphalt production, concrete works, building construction, and plant hire services. Established in 2010, the company has grown into one of Zimbabwe's most trusted infrastructure contractors, delivering high-quality projects for government, mining, industrial, and private sector clients across the region.

With a highly skilled team, modern equipment fleet, and commitment to innovation, safety, and quality, Fossil Contracting continues to drive infrastructure transformation through world-class engineering solutions. The company has successfully executed major projects including highway rehabilitation, bridge construction, asphalt surfacing, and large-scale land development across Zimbabwe.

Zimbabwe's premium civil contracting company.
