Trabablas Interchange Project

Major Infrastructure Development - Harare, Zimbabwe

← Back to Projects

The Trabablas Interchange Project is one of Zimbabwe's landmark infrastructure developments designed to improve traffic flow, reduce congestion, and enhance road safety within Harare. Fossil Contracting is participating in the construction and development of this major transport interchange as part of the country's road modernization initiatives.

The project includes the construction of bridges, road expansions, stormwater drainage systems, water reticulation infrastructure, street lighting, and major earthworks. It also incorporates the development of key connecting roads to improve accessibility and transportation efficiency within the city.

This significant infrastructure project demonstrates Fossil Contracting's capability in delivering large-scale civil engineering and infrastructure projects that contribute to national development, improved mobility, and economic growth.

Project Details

Location: Harare, Zimbabwe

Client: Ministry of Transport

Status: Completed

Completion: 100%

Duration: 2021 - April 2025

Project Scope

  • Bridge construction
  • Road expansions
  • Stormwater drainage systems
  • Water reticulation infrastructure
  • Street lighting installation
  • Major earthworks
  • Connecting roads development
  • Accessibility improvements
