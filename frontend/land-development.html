Land Development Services

Transform raw land into fully prepared, development-ready sites. Comprehensive land development solutions for residential, commercial, mining, and industrial projects.

Site Clearing & Land Preparation

Fossil Contracting provides complete land clearing and preparation services for residential, commercial, mining, and industrial projects. This includes removal of trees, bushes, rocks, debris, and unsuitable soil to prepare land for safe construction and infrastructure development.

Services Include:

  • Bush and vegetation clearing
  • Tree removal and stump extraction
  • Rock breaking and removal
  • Land grading and leveling
  • Soil stabilization
  • Site preparation for roads and buildings

Earthmoving & Excavation

The company offers professional excavation and earthmoving solutions using modern heavy-duty machinery for large-scale infrastructure and mining projects. Our experienced teams deliver precision earthworks with safety and efficiency.

Services Include:

  • Bulk earthworks
  • Excavation for foundations
  • Trenching and drainage excavation
  • Cut and fill operations
  • Soil hauling and compaction
  • Dam and pond excavation

Road & Infrastructure Development

Fossil Contracting specializes in developing access roads, highways, industrial roads, and transport infrastructure for both public and private sector clients. We deliver projects on time and to specification.

Services Include:

  • Road construction
  • Gravel road development
  • Road rehabilitation
  • Drainage systems installation
  • Culvert construction
  • Asphalt preparation and surfacing support

Residential & Commercial Land Development

The company transforms raw land into fully prepared development-ready sites suitable for housing projects, shopping centers, industrial parks, and commercial infrastructure. We handle all aspects of site preparation and servicing.

Services Include:

  • Residential stand development
  • Commercial site servicing
  • Utility trenching
  • Stormwater drainage systems
  • Land subdivision preparation
  • Foundation platform preparation

Mining Site Development

Fossil Contracting supports mining operations through specialized site development and civil works designed for mining environments. We have extensive experience with opencast and underground mining site preparation.

Services Include:

  • Mine access roads
  • Open pit preparation
  • Mining platform construction
  • Waste dump development
  • Heavy equipment access routes
  • Site drainage and stabilization
