Lorraine Drive Road Rehabilitation Project

Urban Infrastructure Development

The Lorraine Drive Road Rehabilitation Project is part of Fossil Contracting's urban road infrastructure development initiatives aimed at improving road quality, accessibility, and traffic movement within Harare. The project focuses on upgrading and rehabilitating the road through asphalt surfacing, drainage improvements, earthworks, and road maintenance works to ensure a safer and more efficient transport network.

The rehabilitation works contribute to reducing road deterioration, improving driving conditions, and enhancing connectivity for residents, businesses, and surrounding communities. The project reflects Fossil Contracting's commitment to delivering durable, high-quality infrastructure solutions that support urban development and economic growth in Zimbabwe.

Project Details

Location: Lorraine Drive, Harare, Zimbabwe

Client: Harare City Council

Status: COMPLETED

Completion: 100%

Year Completed: 2025

Project Scope

  • Asphalt surfacing
  • Drainage improvements
  • Earthworks
  • Road maintenance works
  • Improved accessibility
  • Traffic flow enhancement
  • Urban connectivity
